Taco Seasoning

  • 1 TBS Chili Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Oregano
  • 1/2 tsp Paprika
  • 1 1/2 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 tsp salt (or sea salt)
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1/4 Crushed Red pepper flakes

Mix all ingredients - Makes enough for 2 lbs of protein

Enchilada Seasoning

  • 2 tsp Chili Powder
  • 2 tsp Paprika
  • 2 tsp Corn Starch
  • 1 1/2 tsp Salt (optional)
  • 1 1/2 tsp Onion Powder or dried onion flakes
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 1 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Oregano
    • Optional:
  • 1/2 tsp Ground Coriander
  • 1/4 tsp Ground Cayenne

Mix all ingredients - Makes enough for 1 lb of protein

Chili Seasoning

  • 1 TBS Chili Powder
  • 2 tsp Ground Cumin
  • 1 tsp Ground Oregano
  • 1 TBS Seasoned Salt
  • 1 Dried Chili Pepper, crushed and seeded
  • 1 tsp Minced Onion or Onion powder
  • 1/4 tsp Minced Garlic

Mix all ingredients - Makes enough for 1-2 lbs of protein

BBQ Spice Rub

  • 1/2 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 1/2 Cup Ground Paprika
  • 1 TBS Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 TBS Salt (or less to taste)
  • 1 TBS Chili Powder
  • 1 TBS Garlic Powder
  • 1 TBS Onion Powder
  • 1 tsp Ground Cayenne Powder

Mix all ingredients - Makes enough for 2 lbs of protein

Brown Sugar

  • 1 TBS Molasses for light brown sugar OR 2 TBS Molasses for dark brown sugar
  • 1 Cup white sugar

Mix all ingredients thoroughly until all lumps are removed and texture is consistent.

Buttermilk

  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 1 TBS Lemon Juice

    • Mix together and let sit 10 minutes before using.

Dulce De Leche

  • 14 oz cans of Sweetened Condensed Milk

    • Leave cans closed. Remove labels and adhesives from outside of can. Put cans in a pot big enough to cover the cans with one to two inches of water maintained above the cans throughout the entire cooking time. Simmer over medium high heat for 2 hours for light color, 3 hours for darker color.

Drop Biscuits

  • 2 Cups All Purpose Flour
  • 1 TBS Baking Powder
  • 1 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 Cup Cold, Unsalted Butter, cut into small pieces
  • 3/4 Cup - 1 Cup Milk or Buttermilk

Preheat the oven to 450 F. Whisk together dry ingredients. Cut in cold butter with pastry cutter (or a fork or two butter knives). Add milk and stir until just combined. Mixture will still be slightly lumpy. Drop onto baking sheet using a small measuring cup or ice cream scoop. Bake for about 18 - 22 minutes until golden brown.
